Nebulo 2

An Undertide Project

"Air Quality, Now"

Nebulo was built as an alternative to Singapore's National Environment Agency's myENV app which is clunky with the important data buried within menus.

Aiming to create a seamless and easy experience for people to check for the current Air Quality data, Nebulo allows users to see the relevant information of the current air with just a tap. Forget browsing through menus to find what you need - it really shouldn't have to be this difficult.

We built a backend that works with various government websites/APIs to deliver the most accurate data to our users. Nebulo 2 will be able to work in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Hong Kong.

The recent Nebulo 2 update adds support for multiple geographical location, so we gave it a complete facelift (as seen below).

TL;DR: This app allows people to check for Air Quality data without choking their eyes.
