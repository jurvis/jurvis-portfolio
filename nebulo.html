Nebulo

Personal Project

"Air Quality, Now"

Nebulo is built as an alternative to Singapore's National Environment Agency's myENV app which is clunky with the important data buried within menus.

Aiming to create a seamless and easy experience for people to check for the current Air Quality data, Nebulo allows users to see the relevant information of the current air with just a tap. Forget browsing through menus to find what you need - it really shouldn't have to be this difficult.

We built a backend that scrapes http://aqicn.org/ for PM2.5 and PSI data and stores it to be accessed by the iPhone and Android apps easily. With a refresh scrape of 30 minutes, the user will always know the quality around him.

TL;DR: This app conveniently solved the problem of gathering user feedback by providing the user convenience with a seamless, unobstructive experience.
