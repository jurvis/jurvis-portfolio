RelayPlay

AngelHack AppHack Fall 2013 - SSC Sponsorship Prize Winner

"Personal Stories"

RelayPlay takes up the challenge of coming up with a mobile app that will digitally engage audiences during major sporting events to participate - bringing the games closer to the regional communities during events such as the Women's Tennis Association Championships in 2014 and 28th SEA Games in 2015.

The solution tries to involve people to crowd-write a story. By allowing everyone to contribute two words to a story, each user will then 'bump' on the next device to 'pass the torch' - making the torch relay fun interactive, and personal.

Using the Bump API, we built this solution on iOS and Android for interoperability and had a web backend to help keep track of individual stories being recorded and kept track of the locations where these 'bumps' happened - to be able to visually see how far the "torch" has travelled.

Anyone who has participated in the passing of the "torch" will then always be able to look back at the story he/she has helped contribute to and how far it has grown - this will help encourage virality and people to start and contribute to other stories. Making the experience both personal, engaging and fun during these sporting events.

TL;DR: This app encourages users to "relay" 2 words between device to device to help crowd-write a fun story for the games.
